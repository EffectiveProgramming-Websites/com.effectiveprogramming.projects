While tools like Lean Kit Kanbanize appear to be useful, I wanted something slightly less expensive and literally easier to own. My first attempt at this is to create a Kanban board over the Jekyll platform.

It isn't a gem or downloadable per se as much as it is simply an example of one way to use Jekyll. It lives perpetually in all of my projects and honestly, it will likely differ between instances as I explore different ways to track stories.
